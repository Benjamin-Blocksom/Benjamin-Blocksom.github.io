About

Front-end developer with 2 years experience designing and coding responsive, mobile-first web applications and pages. My focus is on accessibility, HTML e-mail campaign development, and clean UX/UI. I am current with software development practices such as Test-Driven Design (TDD), DRY coding, version control (GIT), and using technical sophistication for troubleshooting and problem solving.

Before coming the world of web development, I spent many years as a translator, working with the Portuguese-English and Spanish-English language pairs. During that time, my work helped companies like HBO, The New York Times, and Citibank. By nature, translation requires a high-level of attention to detail, particularly in editing and formatting. I bring the lessons learned from replicating the form of thousands of documents with me to helping designers bring their creations to life on the web, regardless of the device.
